About-

Uttar Pradesh, often referred to as UP, is a state in northern India. It is the most populous state in India and the fourth-largest Indian state by area. The state is known for its rich cultural heritage, historical significance, and vibrant traditions. Uttar Pradesh is home to several important cities, including the capital city Lucknow, and the historical city of Agra, which houses the iconic Taj Mahal.
